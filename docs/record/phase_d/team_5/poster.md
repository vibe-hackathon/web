# Echoes of Dissent
> Amplify silenced voices and turn every post into a digital protest against censorship.

**Track:** T01

## Problem
Silenced voices and censorship are rampant in many parts of the world. Our platform aims to give a voice to those who are often unheard, ensuring that every post becomes a form of resistance.

## Solution
Our platform uses blockchain for transparency and AI for sentiment analysis to amplify silenced voices globally. Users can post their stories, and the platform will analyze the sentiment and amplify it to a wider audience, ensuring that every voice is heard.

## Tech Stack
- Ethereum blockchain
- TensorFlow for sentiment analysis
- React for frontend
- Node.js for backend
