# FreshFlow
> Optimize supply chains for fresher produce, reduced waste, and improved food accessibility.

**Track:** T01

## Problem
Food waste is a critical global issue, with approximately 1.3 billion tons of food wasted annually. This not only impacts the environment but also hinders food accessibility, especially in underserved communities. Traditional supply chain inefficiencies contribute significantly to this problem.

## Solution
FreshFlow leverages IoT sensors, predictive analytics, and cloud storage to optimize supply chains, ensuring fresher produce reaches consumers faster and reducing waste by up to 50%. By providing 24/7 visibility and actionable insights, FreshFlow enhances food accessibility and sustainability.
