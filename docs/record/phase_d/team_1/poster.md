# MemeHeal
> Turn daily struggles into relatable memes, bringing humor and empathy to mental health conversations.

**Track:** T01 (Best Social Impact)

## Problem
Mental health struggles are often stigmatized and under-discussed. Humor can be a powerful tool to make these conversations more accessible and empathetic.

## Solution
MemeHeal uses AI to generate relatable memes from everyday experiences, turning personal challenges into shared moments of laughter. Our platform includes a chatbot for fact-checking and a community feature to share memes globally.

## Tech Stack
- GPT-4 API for AI generation
- YOLOv8 for meme generation
- AWS for cloud hosting
