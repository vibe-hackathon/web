# HabitHive
> Transform your habits into income streams with friendly, interactive avatars that grow as you succeed.

**Track:** T03

## Problem
People struggle to maintain consistent habits and often miss out on potential income opportunities from their daily activities.

## Solution
HabitHive leverages AI-driven habit tracking, AR avatars, and blockchain rewards to motivate users to turn their habits into income streams.

## Tech Stack
- TensorFlow
- React Native
- Ethereum Blockchain
- ARKit
