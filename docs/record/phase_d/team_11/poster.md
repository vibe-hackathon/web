# Universal Access to Mental Health Care via AI-Powered Virtual Therapists
> Transforming Lives Through Technology

**Track:** T01

## Problem
Despite advancements in mental health care, there remains a global shortage of mental health professionals, leaving many without access to timely and effective care.

## Solution
We will develop an AI-powered virtual therapist platform that provides personalized, accessible, and scalable mental health support, ensuring no one is left behind in the digital age.

## Tech Stack
- TensorFlow
- Flask
- AWS Lambda
- DynamoDB
- NLP Models (BERT, GPT-3)
